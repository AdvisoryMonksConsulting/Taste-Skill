## The outreach message structure (reference)

When writing cold messages, the AI should follow this shape, in your voice:
1. **Personalized opener** — a real, specific detail about them (not "Love your
   content!"). Pull from a recent post, a hiring signal, a funding event.
2. **Relevant value line** — connect that detail to a problem they likely have.
3. **Proof** — one concrete, relevant result from `04-proof.md`.
4. **Soft ask** — a low-pressure question, not a hard pitch.

Short, specific, and relevant beats long and clever. Every time.
