# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

> Purpose: define exactly who to reach so the data layer can build a tight list
> and the AI can write openers that land. No message saves a bad list — you
> cannot personalize your way out of reaching the wrong people. The narrower and
> more painful, the easier everything downstream gets.

## Buying signals (target people showing these *now*)

> FILL IN: The observable triggers that mean a company is in pain *right now*.
> Showing up at the moment of pain converts far better than cold. Examples to
> adapt:
> - Hiring for several of the same role at once (function is scaling / breaking)
> - Raised funding in the last 1–6 months (fresh budget, new problems)
> - Leadership change in the relevant function (new owner wants a quick win)
> - Public complaint / review / job-post language that names your problem

## Activity filter

> FILL IN: Reachability criteria. On LinkedIn, target people who've **posted or
> engaged recently** — someone dormant for months won't see the message. Note
> the minimum activity threshold you'll accept.

## Disqualifiers (who to exclude)

> FILL IN: Just as important as the target. Company types, titles, or situations

## EXAMPLE (fictional — delete when filling in)

**Firmographics:** B2B SaaS, 50–200 employees, Series A–B (raised in last 12
mo), US + Canada, selling annual contracts ($10k–$60k ACV).

**Buyer:** "VP/Head/Director of Customer Success" or "Head of Onboarding."
Reports to COO or CRO (often the economic buyer too at this size). Measured on
net revenue retention and 90-day churn.

**Pain, their words:**
1. "Onboarding takes forever and every customer's setup is bespoke."
2. "We're hiring CSMs just to keep up — it doesn't scale."
3. "Customers churn before they ever see value and the board sees the NRR dip."

**Buying signals:** Posted 2+ CSM/onboarding roles in the last 60 days; raised a
round in the last 6 months; new VP of CS started in the last 90 days.

**Activity filter:** Posted or commented on LinkedIn in the last 30 days.

**Disqualifiers:** Self-serve / PLG-only products (no human onboarding),
sub-$5k ACV (project economics don't work), agencies and consultancies.

**List notes:** Sales Nav — title + headcount + geo + "posted in last 30 days,"
cross-referenced against a job-board scrape for active CSM postings. ~700/campaign.
