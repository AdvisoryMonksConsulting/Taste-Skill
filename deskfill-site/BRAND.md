# DeskFill — Brand Guide

A done-for-you appointment-setting service for B2B recruitment & staffing firms.

## Name & rationale
**DeskFill.** Recruiters run a "desk" and live by their "fill rate" — the name speaks their own language and doubles as *"fill your desk with client meetings."* Signals "we understand your business" before a word of copy is read.

## Tagline
**Client meetings, booked for your desk.**

## Voice
Direct, numbers-led, no fluff. Confident, not clever. We sell results (booked meetings, billings), not design awards. Short sentences. Concrete claims.

## Logo
Wordmark `DeskFill` — "Desk" in ink, "Fill" in emerald — preceded by a rounded-square emerald check mark (inline SVG in the site files). Don't stretch, recolor, or add effects.

## Color palette
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ink | `#0C1322` | Text, headlines, dark sections |
| Emerald | `#10B981` | Primary action, links, accents (signals billings/growth) |
| Emerald (hover) | `#059669` | Button hover, deep accents |
| Slate | `#5B6478` | Body / muted text |
| Cloud | `#F6F8FA` | Soft section backgrounds |
| Line | `#E6E9F0` | Borders |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Base background |

Deliberately **not** generic SaaS-blue. Ink + emerald reads credible and revenue-focused.

## Typography
- **Display / wordmark / headings:** Space Grotesk (500–700)
- **Body / UI:** Inter (400–700)
