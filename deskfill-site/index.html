Questions, answered.

Will this hurt my email reputation or domain?

No. We send from dedicated, separately warmed domains — never your main brand domain. Your reputation is protected.

How fast do we start?

Setup and warmup run in parallel with list-building, so first sends typically go out within days of onboarding.

Do I own the domains and data?

Yes — completely. Everything is set up under your control. If you ever leave, you keep it all.

What counts as a "qualified meeting"?

A booked call with a decision-maker at a company matching the ICP you approved. If it doesn't match, it doesn't count — you tell us within 48 hours.

Why not use an AI SDR instead?

AI senders currently book meetings at or below human rates and churn fast, while risking your domain reputation. We use automation where it helps, but a human qualifies every reply and manages deliverability.
