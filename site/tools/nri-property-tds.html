How to use this

The gap between the default TDS and the tax actually due is the cash a buyer would otherwise withhold and remit to the department — money you reclaim months later, or release up-front with an approved Form 13 certificate.

This estimator is general information, not tax advice. Actual liability depends on indexation, surcharge, reliefs and your residency. Speak with the Pravasi Desk for a precise computation and the Form 13 application.
