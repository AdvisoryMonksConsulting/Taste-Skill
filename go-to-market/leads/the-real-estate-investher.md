## Conversion read (hypotheses — live site is builder-stack; we couldn't crawl it, sandbox-blocked)
They run paid traffic (confirmed; there's literally an SPI episode "The Perfect Funnel for Community Growth" about their funnel). Classic risks for a brand this rich in offers:
- **Too many competing CTAs** above the fold (podcast / free membership / challenge / CON / STRIVE / app / book) → decision paralysis. Our pitch's promise: **one dominant promise + one primary CTA.**
- **Hero promise vague** vs. a sharp outcome ("become a financially-free woman investor — with a community that gets the whole you").
- **Mobile load speed** on a page carrying paid ad spend (our pitch's hook: every slow second costs a click they paid for).
- **Social proof under-leveraged** — 17k members / 100 a week / top podcast belong *in the hero*, not buried.

## Redesign strategy (what the demo demonstrates)
- **One promise, one primary CTA** above the fold: *Join the free community* (their lowest-friction, highest-volume entry — ~100/week already opt in here). Secondary, low-emphasis: *Listen to the podcast.*
- **Lead with proof** in the hero (members, meetups, podcast rank).
- **The 3 pillars** as the organizing section — mirrors how they already think and talk.
- **A single clear "ways to grow" ladder** (Free → PODs → STRIVE → CON) so the many offers read as *one path*, not seven choices.
- **Founders block** (Liz & Andresa) for trust — personal brand is central to this business.
- Warm, premium, editorial aesthetic (deep plum + warm rose/gold on cream) — feminine and aspirational without cliché pink.
