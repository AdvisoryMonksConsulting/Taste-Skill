# Market Intelligence & Launch Plan

_Pre-launch study for Veska (a brand of Advisory Monks Consulting). Markets: US, EU/UK, Singapore, Australia, Africa, India. **Canada — skipped for now** (CASL is opt-in with $10M CAD fines; revisit later via LinkedIn/referrals, not cold email)._

## Market context (validated)
- Global web-design market ≈ **$61–67B (2026)**, ~8.5%/yr; APAC fastest (~17%).
- Share: **North America ~39% · Europe ~28% · APAC ~19%.**
- Benchmark pricing: agency web $1–6k; large sites $36–75k. Veska's $4,950 landing / $11,500 site = **mid-premium** (credible, not cheap).
- Sources: businessresearchinsights.com, vwo.com web-design stats, iscoldemaillegal.com (laws).

## Pricing decision — one global USD price ✅
Run a **single USD price list** ($4,950 / $11,500 / $6–9k retainer / Custom). It works because:
- **Uniform price self-selects the customer** — at ~$4,950 you win funded startups + high-LTV pros everywhere and filter out price-sensitive mass SMBs (a feature, not a bug).
- Your ICP (funded/global-facing/exporters) **exists in every region** and pays USD.
- Simpler, premium-signalling, no per-geo haggling. The **Custom** tier absorbs genuine exceptions.
- Don't discount by geography — **select by geography.**

## India SMB question — keep premium clean
You want India-SMB volume (fast AI/"vibe-coded" sites) without diluting premium Veska. Recommendation:
- **Phase 1 (launch): ignore India SMBs.** Launch Veska as premium-global first so the brand lands cleanly.
- **Phase 2: add a separate, firewalled lane** for India SMB volume — a distinct sub-brand (e.g. "Veska Express" or an AdvisoryMonks-branded SMB web service), separate site, INR pricing, AI-accelerated delivery. Never on the premium Veska domain/funnel.
- This keeps premium positioning intact while still capturing the low-effort, high-volume SMB opportunity later.

## Launch sequence (Tier 1 → Tier 3)
1. **Tier 1 (lead here):** US · UK · Singapore · EU-English — high WTP + cold-email-friendly.
2. **Tier 2:** Australia · Africa-funded — good overlap/low legal risk.
3. **Tier 3:** India (funded startups & GCCs) — then the Phase-2 SMB lane.
- **Skip:** Canada (use LinkedIn/referrals only if pursued).
