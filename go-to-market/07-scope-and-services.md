# Scope & Services

Clear answers to "what exactly do we deliver, what happens after launch, and what do we resell."

## What we deliver — launch-ready, not just HTML
We do **not** hand over loose HTML files. Every engagement ends with a **live, deployed website**:
- Design + conversion copy + build (Next.js, or Webflow when a client wants to self-edit)
- **We deploy it** (Cloudflare Pages / Vercel) and **connect the client's domain** — it goes live
- **On-page & technical SEO foundations baked in:** semantic markup, meta/OG tags, sitemap, schema, fast Core Web Vitals, mobile-first, clean URLs
- Source code handed over (theirs to keep) once final payment clears

> Positioning line for the site/sales: *"We design, build, and launch — your site goes live, not into a zip file."*

## After launch — who handles what
- **Included (free):** 14 days of post-launch bug fixes + a handover walkthrough.
- **On-page SEO:** included at build (it's part of doing the job well). We do **not** sell it as a separate line.
- **Ongoing SEO** (content, keywords, backlinks, rank tracking): **not** in the one-off. Offered as a **retainer** or via a **vetted white-label partner** — we are not a full SEO agency.
- **Care plan (recommended recurring revenue):** hosting + monitoring + small monthly edits, flat monthly fee. Easy upsell, sticky.

## Services matrix — do / add-on / resell / decline

| Tier | Services | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Core (in-house)** | UI/UX design · conversion copy · web build · deploy & launch · on-page/technical SEO · brand/visual identity | This is the product |
| **Add-on / retainer (high margin)** | Growth-design retainer · CRO & A/B testing · landing-page systems · light CMS · email/lifecycle design | Recurring, on-brand |
| **Resell / white-label (take a margin)** | Ongoing SEO content · paid ads (Google/Meta) management · copywriting at scale · photography · hosting & maintenance | Partner delivers; we own the relationship |
| **Refer or decline (protect focus)** | Complex custom web apps / software dev · full performance-marketing · anything off-web | Don't dilute the studio |

> Cross-sell note: accounting / compliance / advisory requests → route to **Advisory Monks Consulting** (the parent), not Veska.

## Default engagement scope (put in proposals)
- **In scope:** agreed pages, design, copy, build, deploy, on-page SEO, 1–2 revision rounds, 14-day bug support.
- **Out of scope (quote separately):** ongoing SEO/content, paid-ads management, integrations beyond the brief, custom app features, additional pages/revisions, photography/illustration licensing.
- **Client provides:** brand assets, content/approvals on time, domain & analytics access.
