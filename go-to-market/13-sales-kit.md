# Sales Kit — calls, replies, proposals

Everything to turn a reply or a booked call into a paid deposit.

## A. When a lead replies "yes / send it"
Reply fast (same day), from the inbox they wrote to:
```
Great — here's a concept of what your new homepage could look like:
{{demo_link}}

This is just the hero direction; the full site keeps your brand and adds clear
booking, trust, and a fast mobile experience. If you like it, the whole thing is
a flat [PRICE], live in 5 days, and the code is yours.

Want to grab 15 minutes this week to walk through it?  {{cal_link}}
```
(`{{demo_link}}` = a `/demos/<client>` page you spin up for them; `{{cal_link}}` = your Cal.com.)

## B. Discovery call script (15 min)
1. **Warm open (1m):** "Thanks for hopping on — saw [their clinic/site], looks like you're [observation]. What made the website worth a look right now?"
2. **Diagnose (4m):** Where do new clients come from? What does the current site do well / badly? How do people book? Any deadline (rebrand, new location, busy season)?
3. **Mirror the gap (2m):** "So the site isn't turning visitors into booked [patients/clients] — that's exactly what we fix."
4. **Pitch the outcome (3m):** premium, conversion-built site, **5 days, flat price, you own it**, on-page SEO + fast mobile included. Show the demo.
5. **Price + terms (2m):** state the flat price plainly. "50% to book your slot, 50% on delivery — and the final half is only due once you're happy."
6. **Close (3m):** "Want me to hold a build slot for you this/next week?" → send deposit invoice on the call.

## C. Objection handling
- **"Too expensive."** → "Totally fair. One new [patient/client] usually covers it — and it's fixed, no surprise invoices. Want to start with just the homepage?"
- **"I'll think about it."** → "Of course. I'll hold a slot 48 hours and send the demo + a one-page scope so it's easy to decide."
- **"I already have a site."** → "You do — this is about it *converting*. Happy to show the 2–3 changes that move the needle most."
- **"Can you do it cheaper?"** → hold price; offer smaller scope (one page) rather than discounting.
- **"How do I know you'll deliver?"** → preview on our link first, 50/50 with the back half on delivery, code handed over — you're never exposed.

## D. Proposal / SOW template (one page)
```
PROPOSAL — [Client name]
Prepared by Veska (a brand of Advisory Monks Consulting)

Goal: A premium, conversion-focused website that turns visitors into booked [clients].

Scope (Launch — flat $4,950, 5 business days):
  • 1 high-converting page: hero, services, trust/reviews, FAQ, booking CTA
  • Conversion copywriting + custom design on your brand
  • Built in Next.js, deployed live on your domain
  • On-page + technical SEO (speed, meta, schema, mobile)
  • 1 revision round · code handed over on final payment

Out of scope (quote separately): extra pages, ongoing SEO, paid ads, photography.

Timeline: starts on deposit; live preview by day 3–4; handover day 5.
Terms: 50% deposit to reserve, 50% on delivery (due once approved). Invoiced in USD
        by Advisory Monks Consulting. Final files & deploy access released on full payment.

Accept: reply "approved" and pay the deposit link to start.
```

## E. Hand-off after close
Deposit clears → build on a `/demos` or preview link → client approves → balance clears →
point their domain + transfer repo. (See `08-getting-paid.md`.)
