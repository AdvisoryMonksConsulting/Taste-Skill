> Built with the `pricing-strategist` and `copywriting` skills.

> Model: solo + AI. Fixed-scope, fixed-price, fixed-timeline. **No hourly. No custom quotes.**

---

## 1. Pricing model decision (the *why*)

| Question (pricing-strategist forcing library) | Answer for this business |
|---|---|
| Outcomes, seats, or usage? | **Outcomes** — clients buy "a page that converts," not your hours. |
| Measurable value metric? | Yes — conversion lift / launch speed. Anchor price to the value of *one* extra customer. |
| Usage variance across clients? | Low — every client needs roughly the same artifact. → **flat productized pricing wins.** |
| Competitor model? | Agencies bill hourly/retainer with 4-week timelines. You differentiate on **fixed price + 5-day delivery**. |
| The ONE upgrade trigger per tier? | Launch tier → *more pages*; Growth tier → *ongoing + CRO*. |

**Verdict:** Productized fixed-price packaging (Good/Better/Best), not hourly. This is the single highest-leverage pricing decision for a solo operator — it removes scoping, estimation, and negotiation, which is where solo service businesses bleed time.

**Risk-reversal block:**

> Half up front to book the slot, half on delivery — and the second half is conditional on you being happy with the work.

**Objection-handlers (FAQ copy):**
- *"Why fixed price?"* — Because you shouldn't gamble on an hourly meter. Scope is defined, so the price is too.

- *"Do I own the code?"* — Completely. It's a clean Next.js repo, handed to you on delivery.
- *"What if I need changes later?"* — Stay on a Growth Partner retainer, or buy another page anytime.

## 4. How to sell it (solo motion)

1. **Anchor on Site ($11.5k)** in conversations — it makes Launch feel like an easy yes and Growth feel natural.
2. **Always quote the package, never the hour.** If asked "what's your rate?" → "I don't bill hourly — the Launch page is a flat $4,950, done in 5 days."
3. **Close on the risk-reversal.** 50/50 split with a conditional back-half removes the buyer's fear.
4. **Every delivery ends with one sentence:** "Want me to keep this optimized monthly?" → that's the Growth upsell.
